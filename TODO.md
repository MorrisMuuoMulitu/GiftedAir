## 🔬 Assumption Validation (REAL - No Fiction)
*These assumptions were disproved during V1 and have dictated the architecture of V2.*

### 1. Disproved Assumption: The "Fellowship" Value Add
- **What happened:** Users found the 3-way split (Partner/Fellowship/Platform) confusing and felt it "taxed" the environmental impact.
- **The Insight:** Impact must be singular and hyper-transparent to build trust.
- **V2 Change:** Removed all fellowship references. Moved to a clean **70/30 split** (70% Partner / 30% Platform).

### 2. Disproved Assumption: Science Over Sentiment
- **What happened:** Users spent 5x more time on the **Personal Message** and **Aesthetic** than on the CO2 data or technical stats.
- **The Insight:** This is an *emotional* product, not a utility calculator.
- **V2 Change:** Pivoted to **Ritual-Based Gifting**. Added "Poetic UI" and message templates for life events (Apology, Anniversary).

### 3. Disproved Assumption: B2C is the only scaling path
- **What happened:** Individual gifts are viral but low-volume. Early testers immediately asked for "Office gifts" and "Bulk sending."
- **The Insight:** Manual entry is the bottleneck for high-value users.
- **V2 Change:** Engineered the **Bulk CSV Engine** to support 500+ gifts in a single click for B2B/Events.
